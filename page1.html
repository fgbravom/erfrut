About us

Who are we?

In 2015, ERFRUT began its journey with a clear focus, the domestic market. Our partners, with experience in various fields such as production, packaging, logistics and marketing, joined forces to enhance a solid, organized and sustainable growth year after year.

Today, thanks to our rigorous work method, we have gone further. We have diversified our commercial options and now export directly to markets as diverse as Taiwan, Latin America, the United States and Europe. This achievement is possible thanks to a passionate and committed team, made up of qualified individuals who love what they do.

At ERFRUT, every step we take is backed by the dedication and passion of our team, and we are proud to share the freshness of our fruits with the world.

THE HEALTHIEST FIBER

What makes the apple a real medicinal food are the phytochemical substances it contains, such as pectin, organic acids, tannins, flavonoids or boron, a mineral in which it is a champion.

Royal Gala

Perfect balance between sugars and acidity

‘Gala’ es una variedad cultivada de manzanas de origen neozelandés, resultante de dos generaciones de cruzamientos controlados realizados por J. H. Kidd. ‘Gala’ se obtuvo del cruzamiento de ‘Kidd’s Orange Red’ × ‘Golden Delicious’.​ Se la llamó ‘Gala’ recién en 1962 y fue liberada para su plantación comercial en 1965

Granny Smith

Green apples with a luminous color

Granny Smith apples are bright green in color, although some may have pink skin. They are crisp, juicy and tart, being excellent for both cooking and raw consumption.

FUJI

The sweetest and crunchiest of all 

Fuji apples are usually large or very large and round, with a dense flesh that is sweeter and crispier than many other apple varieties.

RED DELICIOUS

Crunchy and slightly sweet.

The heart-shaped fruit is bright red in color and sometimes has stripes. Known for having a crisp texture with a slightly mild flavor, this tasty apple excels in fresh, crisp salads.

PINK LADY

The right balance between sugar and acidity 

Famous worldwide for their intense pink color and particularly round shape, they were found to be leaders in the amount of flavonoids, a plant pigment rich in antioxidants that is highly beneficial to human health.

Flavonoids are characterized because they can improve heart disease, relax blood vessels and reduce the risk of high blood pressure, among other benefits.
